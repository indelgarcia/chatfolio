- Complexity Simplified: AI transforms overwhelming financial data into clear, personalized investment strategies through natural conversation.

- Natural Input: Users describe goals conversationally ("save for a house in 5 years"); AI extracts timeline, risk tolerance, and constraints via prompt engineering.

- Instant Analysis: LLM compares portfolio options against user criteria in real-time, explaining trade-offs in plain language instead of static charts.
How AI Capabilities Match the User Problem

6
- Human is serving as an editor to tell the AI what it needs: current financial status, goals, time-horizon, etc. AI is there to advise and clarify what should be done.

- The AI doesn’t have the context of the person’s life, so the human is there to fill in the gaps when necessary so that they can work in tandem for the optimal portfolio.

- Combining the human’s real-world needs with the AI’s vast knowledge in personal finance.
