MAIN TASKS SUPPORTED
TASK ANALYSIS
01
🎯  Goal Elicitation
User states investment intent in plain English. System classifies goal (retirement, house, etc.) and tags it live on the sidebar.
02
📊  Risk Profiling
Conversational scenario questions replace dry risk tolerance surveys. AI interprets nuanced responses and confirms interpretation.
03
💼  Portfolio Generation
After profiling, the system generates a personalized ETF allocation with adjustable risk steering and allocation breakdown.
04
🔄  Portfolio Review & Adjustment
User can accept, adjust, get an AI explanation, or compare alternative allocations — all without leaving the interface.
05
👁️  Live Profile Tracking
A persistent sidebar shows the user's growing profile in real-time, so they always know what's been captured.
06
🔮  What-If Simulation
Users can toggle risk steering or modify allocations and see how outcomes change before committing.

KEY MECHANISMS
Confirmation Loop
AI paraphrases ambiguous answers and asks the user to confirm or clarify before storing to profile.
Persistent Profile State
Sidebar shows live state — users see their profile grow in real time, creating a sense of progress.
Risk Steering Slider
Post-generation, users can drag risk posture from conservative to aggressive and watch allocation shift.
Tabbed Review Layer
Recommendation vs. What If? tabs let users explore without committing — reducing anxiety around decisions.

DESIGN RATIONALE
WHY WE BUILT IT THIS WAY
Conversation over Forms
Research shows users abandon financial forms at high rates. Dialogue reduces perceived cognitive load and mirrors how people discuss money with a human advisor.
⚖ Slightly longer onboarding — offset by higher completion rates and user trust.
Live Profile Sidebar
Dual-panel design gives users a mental model of what the system 'knows.' It addresses anxiety that information is lost between turns.
⚖ Requires more screen real estate — designed for desktop-first use.
Confirmation Loops for Ambiguity
NLP misclassification of financial intent is high-stakes. The AI explicitly surfaces its interpretation and asks for confirmation before committing.
⚖ Adds one extra turn in ambiguous cases — worth it for accuracy.
Defer Commitment at Review
Four distinct post-generation actions (Accept / Adjust / Explain / Compare) let users explore before committing, reducing decision anxiety.
⚖ More UI complexity — addressed by clear visual hierarchy and labeled CTAs.
Risk Steering Slider
Allows continuous control post-generation without restarting the whole flow. Empowers users who want to override the AI recommendation.
⚖ Risk of uninformed override — mitigated by Explain function.
ETF-based Recommendations
Low-cost index ETFs (VTI, VXUS, etc.) are evidence-based defaults that reduce portfolio cost and complexity for beginner investors.
⚖ Not suitable for active traders — ChatFolio is explicitly designed for passive, long-term investors.
